The Start

Envisioning the growing demand of easily ready to cook, hygienic and nutritious food, in the rapidly growing metropolitan areas of Pakistan, Dawn Foods was launched in 2008 to cater to this trend.

A household Name

As a standout in public perception, the trust and acceptance people show to Dawn Foods is reflected in its significant and ever-growing market share in a now very competitive Frozen Food market. And with this trust Dawn Foods aims to become an unmatchable brand in its category.

True Variety

Having started with Frozen Dough Products (Parathas), Dawn Foods now offers a wholesome range of Ready-to-Cook and Ready-to-Eat products with focus on Chicken, Vegetable and Wheat as categories. But to always meet public acclaim, Dawn Foods will keep on expanding its product range according to food standards and ongoing trends.

Ever Evolving

To deliver the best customer satisfaction, Dawn Foods established its production facilities and manufacturing process in line with international standards, and continues to upgrade its production technology to deliver exceptional quality and freshness.

Strong Roots

Bringing the Knowledge and perseverance of up to 40 years of its parent company, Dawn Bread to the play, Dawn Foods understands the demands of Pakistani consumer market very well. And following the footsteps of its parent company Dawn Foods always delivers quality and affordability keeping its pride of quality claim high, so each of their product can be easily accepted by every Pakistani.
